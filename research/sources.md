# Research Sources — Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS

## Why I Chose This Topic
Cold outreach is the most measurable, repeatable growth channel for B2B SaaS.
Unlike content or ads, results are directly tied to message quality and process —
making it ideal for building a replicable playbook grounded in practitioner data.

## Expert Selection Criteria
- Actively practises cold outreach (runs campaigns, leads SDR teams, or owns an agency)
- Posts specific data — reply rates, meeting counts, sequence structures
- Content published in 2024–2025
- Mix of LinkedIn-native creators and YouTube educators
